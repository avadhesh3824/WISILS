Welcome to WiSILS-2024

"Asian Symposium Intense Laser (WiSILS-2024): Unraveling the Frontiers of Laser-Driven Phenomena"
Laser Plasma Research Facilities in India: TIFR Mumbai | IIT Hyderabad | TIFR Hyderabad | RRCAT Indore

Objectives of WiSILS-2024

Embark on a transformative journey into the cutting-edge realm of Intense Laser Science at our Winter School. This comprehensive delves into High and Ultra-high Laser Intensity Science, exploring its diverse applications across multiple disciplines. Participants will be exposed to High Harmonic Generation, Attosecond Science,Laser-Driven Particle Acceleration, Ultrafast Dynamics of Matter at High Energy Density on the experimental, theoretical and computational fronts.

Key Themes of the School

  • High to Ultra-high Laser Intensity Science: Understand the principles and applications of lasers operating at extreme intensities, transforming fields from materials science to nuclear physics.
  • High Harmonic Generation and Attosecond Science: We will delve into the fascinating world of attosecond physics(Nobel Physics 2023) , enabling the investigation of ultrafast electronic processes at an unprecedented timescale.
  • Laser-Driven Particle Acceleration: Explore the forefront of accelerator technology, harnessing intense laser pulses to propel particles to near light speeds with implications for medicine, industry, and fundamental physics.
  • Ultrafast Plasma Dynamics: Unravel the complex behavior of plasmas under extreme conditions, unlocking insights into astrophysical phenomena and advanced fusion energy concepts.
  • Computational Plasma Physics: Master the computational tools that underpin our understanding of plasma interactions, enabling accurate simulations of laser-plasma interactions.

Highlights

  • World-Class Faculty: Learn from leading experts, pioneers in their respective fields, who have shaped the landscape of Intense Laser Science.
  • Hands-On Workshops: Engage in practical sessions, gaining valuable experience in computation, numerical simulations, and data analysis.
  • Interdisciplinary Networking: Foster collaborations with fellow participants from diverse backgrounds, forging connections that transcend disciplinary boundaries.
